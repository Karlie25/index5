Reading Response 1

How to Crit?

Reading:
How to Crit by Mitch Goldstein

1. What are some challenges you face when giving a critique?
When giving the critique, the biggest challenge that I face is how I can help the people I critique not feeling belittle instead feeling good to make their works better. I agreed with Mitch Goldstein in "How to crit" that crit should have goals on the expectations because it helps the suggestions and ideas go in the right directions. Sometimes, I afraid of misleading the person that I give critique to going on the wrong way because I am not sure whether or not they fully understand what I am trying to say.
2. How do you handle a critique that you disagree with?
When I receive a critique that I disagree with, I will tell that person why I do not think the way they think. Additionally, I will ask them some questions to clarify and make sure they understand the reasons why I do what I do. On the other hand, there is a chance that I misunderstand their point of view, so by asking questions, and I would learn more about their opinions. I understand that everyone has their own way of thinking. At the end of the discussion, I will do some reflections of what are the best fit for my own practices.
