Exercise 1: Self-Portrait.

About me.

Hi, my name is Karlie. This fall, I am going to my third year in a graphic design program.
I use the blob mixer tool to represent myself. I am the type of person that gets anxious over something minor. For example, if a project does not go the way I planned, I feel panic, feel stressed when I have to do a presentation in front of the class, etc. That’s why I set up the speed fast and added some waves to represent my emotion when the anxiety attacks. However, I see anxiety as an opportunity for me to push myself moving forwards, so I don’t think it is a bad thing at all. I used yellow to express the optimistic side of my personality and red in the background to show how both my anxiety and optimism cohabitate.
