CSS Typeface

Process


Sketch

I started this project by sketching randomly any ideas I had in my mind.
First, I tried to combine geometric shapes to create my alphabet. However, I was not fond of this idea much because the letters came out quite rough, in my opinion. Because of that, I played with more organic shapes and lines to see if the results have more elegant looks.

I realized if I shorten the height of letters while keeping the exact width, the appearance of each letter look cleaner and more modern.

Reference

When I figured out what style of the alphabet I want it to look like, I went on Google to search for inspiration.

I decided to do the whole alphabet in lowercase after looking at some references on the internet. Lowercase seems to fit with the cute, rounded feel more than uppercase.


Compare with


In the end, I decided to combined the two styles I got in the sketching stage to complete the look of my typeface. I think the pointy edges with the rounded, soft feel produced a nice contrast.
